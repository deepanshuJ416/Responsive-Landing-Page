What is Ladakh Famous for?

Ladakh is most famous for breathtaking landscapes, the crystal clear skies, the highest mountain passes, thrilling adventure activities, Buddhist Monasteries and festivals.Ladakh is famous for many reasons, including but not limited to: the pristine natural beauty, the cold desert Landscape, beautiful valleys, magnificent blue lakes, spectacular mountain hugging monasteries, high mountain passes, and riverside villages, which features incredible beauty of this place.
